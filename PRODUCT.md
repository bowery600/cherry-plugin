# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
- **Early-stage Founders**: Entrepreneurs looking to raise seed capital in New England. They seek a professional, responsive, and supportive investor group.
- **Angel Investors / Members**: High-net-worth individuals, active operators, and industry leaders looking to join a high-quality, collaborative angel network.

## Product Purpose
- Provide a modern, trustworthy, and visually stunning digital home for Cherrystone Angel Group.
- Streamline the application process for founders and onboarding for new members.
- Celebrate Cherrystone's 20-year history of patient, collaborative investing in New England.

## Brand Personality
- **Refined & Trustworthy**: Established, professional, and sophisticated, reflecting decades of successful investing.
- **Organic & Human**: Centered on people, collaboration, and New England heritage rather than cold, transactional metrics.
- **Forward-Looking / Translucent**: High-tech yet warm, using clean typography, glassmorphism, and gentle fluid motion to feel state-of-the-art.

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS templates with bright purple/blue gradient buttons on plain white backgrounds.
- High-contrast, dark mode "gaming" or "crypto" designs that look overly aggressive or tech-heavy.
- Heavy, static layouts that feel like outdated corporate portals.

## Design Principles
- **Classic Typography Meets Modern Fluidity**: Bold editorial serif titles (EB Garamond) paired with highly legible, clean sans-serif text (Inter Tight) and monospace details (JetBrains Mono).
- **Physical Translucency**: Visual layers built on frosted glass elements (`backdrop-filter`) floating above a warm paper canvas.
- **Restrained Motion**: Interactive elements that react smoothly to scroll and hover, elevating the user experience without ever overwhelming or slowing down the device.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- Contrast ratios of at least 4.5:1 for body copy against the warm paper background or glass panels.
- Full support for `prefers-reduced-motion` settings, falling back to static visual layouts.
- Touch target sizes of at least 44px for all mobile interactive elements.
