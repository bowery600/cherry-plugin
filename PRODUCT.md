# Product

## Register

brand

## Users
Founders seeking investment, current angel investors, and prospective investors. They use the site to evaluate the firm, submit pitch applications, or access confidential resources inside the member portal.

## Product Purpose
To showcase Cherrystone Angel Group's investments and team, to attract high-quality startups, and to provide a secure and efficient member portal for due diligence and internal documents.

## Brand Personality
Professional, established, and confident. The tone is authoritative but accessible to serious founders.

## Anti-references
SaaS-style startup dashboards, generic "warm/beige" templates without character, over-animated trendy web3 pages.

## Design Principles
- "Show, don't tell": Let the portfolio and member directory speak to the firm's quality.
- "Expert confidence": Typography and spacing should feel solid, intentional, and clean.
- "Frictionless utility": The member portal should be instantly usable without learning curves.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
WCAG AA compliance for contrast. High legibility for text-heavy documents and directories.
